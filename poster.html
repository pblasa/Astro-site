Astrology Posters (DIN A1 Format)



Most of us are lost in the woods. The woods of life that is. Until one day Universe/Source/God comes knocking on our door. Are we ready for the things that life wants us to take on?

Through the magic of astrology, we seek to understand our greater role in living this life. We need a map and astrology is the map of our destiny, and how to take the wheel before autopilots take us to uncertain destinies. This A1 sized poster (60 x 84 cm) is a rundown of all the different aspects of astrology, a short hand summary of all these parameters in reading a chart.

Printout your birthchart using a website like astro.com or astro-seek.com and generate your birth chart. Then use this poster to reveal the various aspects of you.
